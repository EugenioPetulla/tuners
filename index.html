Everything started from Firefox OS project

At the beginning of 2016 Mozilla closed it's mobile experience with the project Firefox OS. I had some quite popular apps on the marketplace so I decided to put it on GitHub and let them die with honour. After some months I've been invited to speak in Tirana, at the OSCAL, where I met a lot of other Mozillian developers that were very sad about the end of the project like me and they came with the solution: put everything online as a service for other music players like me.
